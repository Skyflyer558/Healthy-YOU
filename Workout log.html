You can track your work in many forms, according to what type of training you did.

At the Gym

Here is your progress sheet for your muscle building workouts. Wether you train through weight machines, free weights.

Home Workout

That you'd be at the gym or at home, this is where you record your own bodyweight workout. Burpees, Squats, lunges, push-ups... It's the strenghtening and cardio building at the same time. You can also You can even record your water intakes, by checking those water intake boxes. Staying hydrated, even in winter, is very important!

Cardio and flexibility training

Here is where you track for your cardio routines, that be running, walking, cycling, cross country skiing, snowshoeing, your stretchings, like Yoga, pilates, some weight training, aerobic routines and even Yoga and Pilates exercises.
